About ACM VIT-AP

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society. At VIT Amaravati, ACM strives to advance computing as a science and profession by organizing various events, workshops, and hackathons. Our goal is to inspire young minds and nurture their computing skills for future innovation.
